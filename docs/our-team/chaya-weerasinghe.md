# Chaya Weerasinghe

![Chaya Weerasinghe](../images/chaya-weerasinghe.jpg){ .person }

Chaya Weerasinghe is a postdoctoral researcher in Bayesian statistics at the Department of Infectious Diseases, University of Melbourne, based at the Peter Doherty Institute for Infection and Immunity. Her current research focuses on the development of Bayesian methods for infectious disease research, with a particular emphasis on clinical trials and controlled human infection models. Her broader research interests include Bayesian inference and forecasting, model misspecification, and approximate Bayesian methods.
