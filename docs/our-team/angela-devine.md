# A/Prof Angela Devine

![A/Prof Angela Devine](../images/angela-devine.jpg){ .person }

A/Prof Angela Devine is a Health Economist at Menzies School of Health Research and Melbourne School of Population and Global Health, University of Melbourne. Her current research focuses on the costs and cost-effectiveness of strategies for the diagnosis, treatment, and control of malaria. Other research interests include the economic evaluation of infectious diseases and the measurement and valuation of productivity losses.
