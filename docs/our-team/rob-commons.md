# A/Prof Rob Commons

![A/Prof Rob Commons](../images/rob-commons.jpg){ .person }

Dr Rob Commons is an infectious diseases and general medicine physician at Ballarat Health Services and a Senior Research Officer at Menzies School of Health Research who works closely with the team of data scientists at qMalaria. His research focuses on ways to optimise P. vivax radical cure regimens to improve efficacy and reduce haemolysis, including undertaking large pooled individual patient data meta-analyses through an extended collaboration with the WorldWide Antimalarial Resistance Network (WWARN).
