# A/Prof Emily Karahalios

![A/Prof Emily Karahalios](../images/emily-karahalios.jpg){ .person }

Dr Emily Karahalios is a Research Fellow (biostatistician) based in the Biostatistics Units within the Melbourne School of Population and Global Health, The University of Melbourne, and the School of Public Health and Preventive Medicine, Monash University. Her primary research interests include methods to synthesise evidence from multiple treatments (also known as network meta-analysis) and ways to deal with missing data in longitudinal studies.
