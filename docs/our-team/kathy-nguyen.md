# Kathy Nguyen

![Kathy Nguyen](../images/kathy-nguyen.jpg){ .person }

Kathy Nguyen is a Research Assistant at the Centre for Epidemiology and Biostatistics, within the Melbourne School of Population and Global Health, University of Melbourne. Kathy completed both her undergraduate and post-graduate studies (Bachelor of Biomedicine and Masters of Public Health) at the University of Melbourne. 

Her research focus and work in Plasmodium vivax malaria mainly consists of assisting the research team through drafting statistical analysis plans, contributing to study protocols and undertaking data preparation and analysis using statistical software such as Stata and R, as well as assisting with project management activities.
